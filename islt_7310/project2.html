Food and Family


As an accomplished Operations Manager, I bring extensive expertise in overseeing and optimizing catering operations to ensure seamless event execution. With a focus on meticulous planning, team leadership, and client satisfaction, I consistently drive success in the following key areas:


Comprehensive Event Management

I excel in managing all facets of catering operations, from meticulous logistical planning to flawless execution. By coordinating tasks and pre-event logistics, I ensure that every event runs smoothly, meeting and exceeding client expectations. My responsibilities encompass strategic purchasing, client communication, outreach, marketing initiatives, and in-depth post-event analysis to continually refine and improve services.


HR Leadership and Employee Development

In addition to event logistics, I oversee HR functions, providing valuable support to employees and managing payroll processing. My leadership extends to training and mentoring staff members, fostering a culture of growth and improvement. Through effective coaching and development, I've consistently elevated team efficiency and productivity.


Vendor and Client Collaboration

Collaboration lies at the heart of my approach. I maintain strong relationships with vendors and suppliers, ensuring a reliable network of resources. My dedication to client satisfaction translates into seamless event execution and consistently high service levels. Clients can rely on my commitment to delivering memorable experiences that align with their unique preferences and expectations.


By combining these skills and experiences, I've achieved remarkable success in optimizing catering operations, delivering exceptional client experiences, and fostering a dynamic and productive team. My portfolio reflects my commitment to excellence in every aspect of event management.
